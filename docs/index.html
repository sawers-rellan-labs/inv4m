inv4m Analysis Pipeline

Maize Inv4m chromosomal inversion and phosphorus stress response

Foundation Analyses

Spatial Correction

Spatial correction for field phenotypes using SpATS models to account for field heterogeneity.

Differential Gene Expression

RNA-seq analysis identifying differentially expressed genes under the leaf × phosphorus treatment interaction model.

Differential Lipid Analysis

Lipidomics analysis of membrane remodeling under phosphorus stress using MS-DIAL data.

Phenotype Analyses

Growth Curves

Longitudinal growth analysis comparing Inv4m and control genotypes under phosphorus treatments.

Ionome Profiling

Mineral nutrient analysis (P, K, Ca, Mg, Fe, Zn, etc.) across genotypes and treatments.

Phenotype Marginal Means

Estimated marginal means for phenotypic traits with statistical comparisons.

Enrichment Analyses

GO Enrichment

Gene Ontology enrichment analysis of differentially expressed genes.

KEGG Pathway Enrichment

KEGG pathway analysis linking DEGs to metabolic and signaling pathways.

LION Lipid Enrichment

Lipid ontology enrichment for differentially abundant lipids.

Synthesis

Transcription Indices

Gene expression indices for photosynthesis, senescence, and stress response pathways.

Volcano Plots

Visualization of differential expression results with annotated significant genes.
